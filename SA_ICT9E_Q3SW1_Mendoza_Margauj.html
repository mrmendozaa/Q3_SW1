TEEN TITANS GO



SYPNOSIS: It is a series of a group of 4 young heroes who make the world a better place to settle in.


STARFIRE

Starfire is also known as Princess Koriand'r who originally lives in Tamaranea and is known for her power to absorb ultraviolet radiation (UV RAYS).

CYBORG

Cyborg is a half cybernetic and half human character in this series, thus he is very knowledgeable about technology because he himself is made from it.

BEAST BOY

Beast boy's history is quite interesting as he got his powers from a serum that came from a green monkey, thus his green color. He is a character that can transform into different objects or animals he may transform into an eagle, a glass, or even water!

ROBIN

Robin is considered as the main character of the series, he is in fact the leader of the titans. He has no supernatural powers but he depends on his physical abilities and his technological devices.

RAVEN

If Cyborg is a human-cybernetic hybrid, Raven on the other hand is a human-demon hybrid. She is an empath, and can also read the emotions of other people and control them which makes her telepathic.
